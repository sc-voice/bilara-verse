Ekakanipāta

15. Uttarātherīgāthā

Verses of the Senior Nuns

The Book of the Ones

1.15. Uttarā (1st)

Thig1.15:1.1

“Kāyena saṁvutā āsiṁ,
vācāya uda cetasā;
Samūlaṁ taṇhamabbuyha,
sītibhūtāmhi nibbutā”ti.

I was restrained
in body, speech, and mind.
Having plucked out craving root and all,
I’m cooled and quenched.
