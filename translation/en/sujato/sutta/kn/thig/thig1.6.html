6. Dhīrātherīgāthā

Verses of the Senior Nuns

The Book of the Ones

1.6. Dhīrā

Thig1.6:1.1

“Dhīre nirodhaṁ phusehi,
Saññāvūpasamaṁ sukhaṁ;
Ārādhayāhi nibbānaṁ,
Yogakkhemamanuttaran”ti.

Dhīrā, touch cessation,
the blissful stilling of perception.
Win extinguishment,
the supreme sanctuary.
