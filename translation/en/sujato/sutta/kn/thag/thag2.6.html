The Book of the Twos

Chapter One

2.6. Meḷajina

Thag2.6:1.1

“Yadāhaṁ dhammamassosiṁ,
bhāsamānassa satthuno;
Na kaṅkhamabhijānāmi,
sabbaññūaparājite.

When I heard the Teacher
speaking Dhamma,
I wasn’t aware of any doubt
in the all-knowing, unconquered one,
