Verses of the Senior Monks

The Book of the Ones

Chapter Nine

1.81. Samitigutta

Thag1.81:1.1

“Yaṁ mayā pakataṁ pāpaṁ,
pubbe aññāsu jātisu;
Idheva taṁ vedanīyaṁ,
vatthu aññaṁ na vijjatī”ti.
… Samitigutto thero ….

Whatever bad things I’ve done
in previous lives,
are to be experienced right here,
not in any other place.
