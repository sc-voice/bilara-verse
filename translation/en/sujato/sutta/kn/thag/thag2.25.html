Verses of the Senior Monks

The Book of the Twos

Chapter Three

2.25. Vītasoka

Thag2.25:1.1

“Kese me olikhissanti,
kappako upasaṅkami;
Tato ādāsamādāya,
sarīraṁ paccavekkhisaṁ.

The barber approached
to shave my head.
I picked up a mirror
and examined my body.
