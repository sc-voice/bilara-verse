1.68. Ekudāniyattheragāthā

Verses of the Senior Monks

The Book of the Ones

Chapter Seven

1.68. Ekudāniya

Thag1.68:1.1

“Adhicetaso appamajjato,
Munino monapathesu sikkhato;
Sokā na bhavanti tādino,
Upasantassa sadā satīmato”ti.
… Ekudāniyo thero ….

A sage of higher consciousness, diligent,
training in the ways to sagacity:
there are no sorrows for such a one,
calm and ever mindful.
