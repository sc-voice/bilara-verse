1.32. Suppiyattheragāthā

Verses of the Senior Monks

The Book of the Ones

Chapter Four

1.32. Suppiya

Thag1.32:1.1

“Ajaraṁ jīramānena,
tappamānena nibbutiṁ;
Nimiyaṁ paramaṁ santiṁ,
yogakkhemaṁ anuttaran”ti.
… Suppiyo thero ….

I’ll swap old age for the unaging,
burning for extinguishment—
the ultimate peace,
the supreme sanctuary.
