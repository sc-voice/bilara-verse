Verses of the Senior Monks

The Book of the Ones

Chapter Eleven

1.102. Setuccha

Thag1.102:1.1

“Mānena vañcitāse,
Saṅkhāresu saṅkilissamānāse;
Lābhālābhena mathitā,
Samādhiṁ nādhigacchantī”ti.
… Setuccho thero ….

Deceived by conceit,
defiled among conditions,
oppressed by gain and loss,
they don’t reach immersion.
