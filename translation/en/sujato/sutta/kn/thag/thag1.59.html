Verses of the Senior Monks

The Book of the Ones

Chapter Six

1.59. Kosalavihārin

Thag1.59:1.1

“Saddhāyāhaṁ pabbajito,
araññe me kuṭikā katā;
Appamatto ca ātāpī,
sampajāno patissato”ti.
… Kosalavihāritthero ….

I went forth out of faith
and built a little hut in the wilderness.
I’m heedful, ardent,
aware, and mindful.
