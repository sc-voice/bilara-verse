1.61. Vappattheragāthā

Verses of the Senior Monks

The Book of the Ones

Chapter Seven

1.61. Vappa

Thag1.61:1.1

“Passati passo passantaṁ,
apassantañca passati;
Apassanto apassantaṁ,
passantañca na passatī”ti.
… Vappo thero ….

One who sees
sees those who see and those who don’t.
One who doesn’t see
sees neither.
